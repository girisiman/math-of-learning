Weekly Course Schedule

Week Event Details
Week 1

🎯 Theme: "The Matrix Reloaded: Vectors, Spaces, and All That Jazz"

📖 Lecture: Introduction to the Course & Linear Algebra for Learning

🔍 Topics: Vectors, norms, inner products, projections, matrix operations
Week 2

🎯 Theme: "Dice, Data & Destiny: Probabilistic Learning Begins!"

📖 Lecture: Probability and Generalization in Learning

🔍 Topics: Random variables, distributions, expectations, law of large numbers, generalization error
Week 3

🎯 Theme: "PAC-Man: Eat Errors, Learn Probably!"

📖 Lecture: Statistical Learning Theory – Part I

🔍 Topics: Empirical Risk Minimization, PAC learning framework, hypothesis classes
Week 4

🎯 Theme: "VC Avengers: Complexity Wars"

📖 Lecture: Learning Theory – Part II

🔍 Topics: VC Dimension, Rademacher Complexity, Overfitting vs. Underfitting
Week 5

🎯 Theme: "Don’t Overfit It! The Regularization Games"

📖 Lecture: Regularization and Generalization

🔍 Topics: L1/L2 regularization, bias-variance tradeoff, norm-based control of models
Week 6

🎯 Theme: "Information is Power: Learning Like a Spy!"

📖 Lecture: Information Theory and Learning

🔍 Topics: Entropy, KL divergence, mutual information, information bottlenecks
Week 7

🎯 Theme: "Descent into Madness: The Gradient Chronicles"

📖 Lecture: Basic Optimization for Learning

🔍 Topics: Gradient descent, convexity, convergence, learning rates, stochastic vs. batch methods

Frequently Asked Questions

This course is intended for Level 5 and Level 6 students in Computer Science or related disciplines, with a good grasp of linear algebra, calculus, and probability.

The course consists of weekly lectures and accompanying problem-solving sessions focused on key mathematical concepts underlying machine learning.

The primary emphasis is on mathematical foundations. However, some topics may involve numerical demonstrations or simulations, preferably in Python.

This is currently a non-credit enrichment course. However, it significantly enhances theoretical preparation for research or advanced study in machine learning.

Auditing may be permitted based on availability and prior discussion with the course instructor. Priority will be given to registered students.
